Coconut Chicken Noodle Soup (ohn no khao swè)

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ pound chicken, boned, skinned and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • ½ teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • Sea salt
  • ¼ cup peanut oil
  • 1 medium size onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • ¼ cup roasted chickpea flour
  • ⅔ cup coconut milk
  • 5 peeled shallot lobes
  • 2 Hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges 
  • ¾ pound egg noodles

Garnishes (optional):

  • 1 tablespoons ground dried red chilies, pan roasted until fragrant
  • 2 Hard-boiled eggs, sliced them thinly
  • Handful of rice noodles, deep-fried
  • 1 large sweet or red onion, halved, cut into thin crescents, and soaked in water
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

DIRECTIONS

Step 1: Cook the Chicken

  1. Knead the chicken together with the fish sauce, turmeric and a pinch of salt.
  2. Place a medium saucepan over medium-high heat and add the oil.
  3. Put the finely chopped onions in after the oil is heated. Stir often until onion is softened and translucent.
  4. Add the finely chopped garlic and stirred for 1 more minute.
  5. Stir in the paprika, then the chicken. Cook, stirring often, until the inside of chicken cubes are cooked.

Step 2: Make Chicken Coconut Soup

  1. In a large pot, boil the chicken broth.
  2. Whisk the chickpea flour in 1/2 cup warm water until smooth and stir into the broth once the broth is boil. Stir often until the broth is boiling again.
  3. Add the chicken to the broth, and stir in the coconut milk. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat.
  4. Add the shallot lobes and simmer until just cooked through.
  5. Add half of thinly sliced egg and stir.
  6. Season to taste.

Step 3: Cook Noodle

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add noddles and cook until tender.
  3. Drain and rinse in cold water
  4. Drizzle a little oil and mix to keep the noodles from sticking

To serve, place a handful of noodles in a bowl and ladle on a generous amount of coconut chicken soup. Garnish as desired with hard-boiled egg, red chili power, soaked onion, lime, fish sauce, and fried noodles.
